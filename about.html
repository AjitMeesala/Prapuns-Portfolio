A Spark Ignited

Prapun’s story isn't just about a career shift, it's a journey with over 100,000 voices. He is an HR professional, who witnessed the hopes and dreams of students and seasoned professionals alike, each one a spark waiting to ignite. This ignited a fire within him, leading to the vibrant Vizag startup ecosystem in 2015.

Immersed in the Startup World

Prapun Immersed himself in the vibrant Vizag startup ecosystem for over eight years, and had collaborated with over 1,000 diverse individuals, co-founders, students and industry leaders soaking up the collective wisdom of this dynamic world.

Learning and Growth

To fuel his thirst for knowledge, he absorbed insights from over 200 sessions by thought leaders at APIS, NASSCOM, T-HUB, A-HUB, IIMV, Wadhwani Foundation, Villgro and countless others. Boot camps, programming workshops, interactive sessions and personalized internships crafted by Prapun, helped college students to unlock their potential. His NCC Air Wing Cadet spirit, aero-modeling medals and testament to his dedication added a unique dimension to his workshops.

Evolution and Transformation

Prapun invested in upgrading his skills, exploring content creation, growth hacking, generative AI and strategic thinking. This evolution transformed him into an educator, facilitator, mentor, career advisor and speaker making each role a vibrant facet of his identity.

Journey Inwards

The ultimate lessons, came from exploring Vipassana and inner engineering awakened a deep sense of mindfulness which grounding him in the present moment.

Gratitude and Acknowledgment

This journey wouldn't be complete without mentioning the incredible individuals who shaped him - Soma Sundar sir, Irfan Ansari, Dheeraj Kota, and Rohini Kumar Barla. He learned a lot from each one of them.

A Bridge to Dreams

Today, Prapun is not just an ex-HR professional or an entrepreneur. He is a bridge between dreams and reality, a testament to the power of curiosity and a grateful student of life's endless lessons. So, if you're looking for inspiration, a roadmap to growth, or simply someone to share your entrepreneurial dreams, let's connect!

Education and Work Experience

Prapun is an alumni of Andhra University, Mrs. A.V.N. College and St Aloysius' Anglo-Indian High School. Worked for Product Clients, Nokia, Reliance, TATA, IBM Daksh and the US Certification Process.

Startup Involvement

Metaintrospec Innovators Pvt. Ltd, an incubated startup in the Andhra Pradesh Innovation Society(APIS), Visakhapatnam.
